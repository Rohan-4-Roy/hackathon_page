AI for Collaborative Assessment in STEM

Transforming evaluation through intelligent, transparent, and fair assessment systems

Theme Overview

This theme explores how AI systems can support peer-based, group, and project-led assessment in STEM education. The focus is on fairness, transparency, and feedback quality, enabling learners and instructors to evaluate process, contribution, and learning outcomes beyond individual, exam-centric metrics.
